  Key Features of the Visualization:
   1. Growth Trend Momentum (Top Chart):
       * Consensus vs. Inertia: Plots the real-time success rate (consensus) against the historical trend (inertia).
       * Growth Breakouts: Highlights points where lyapunov_delta > 0, signaling a "Bifurcation" or a positive micro-moment where the system is breaking away from
         historical norms into a new growth phase.
       * Performance Zones: Shaded areas clearly distinguish between overperforming and underperforming intervals.

   2. Social Role Analysis (Bottom Chart):
       * Dynamic Segmentation: A stacked area chart tracks the distribution of Followers (predictable users), Outliers (unexpected successes/failures), and Caos (erratic
         behavior) over time. This allows you to see if growth is being driven by a reliable "Follower" base or volatile "Outliers."

   3. Actionable Diagnostics:
       * Direct Verdict: The visual includes an automated annotation box that displays the current momentum_verdict and the recommended action_plan (e.g., "SCALE: Double
