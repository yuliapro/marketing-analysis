
  Dashboard Insights:
   1. Flow Momentum (Top): Compares the real-time efficiency (Consensus) of every funnel level against its historical norm (Inertia). It visualizes the "energy" of each transition.
   2. Divergence Analysis (Middle-Left): Tracks anomalies. Red shading indicates a funnel level is "heating up" (performing better than history), while Blue shading indicates a cooling trend or efficiency drop.
   3. Friction Map (Middle-Right): A bar chart that immediately identifies which funnel level has the highest resistance (Difficulty), allowing for rapid prioritization of UX fixes.
   4. System Stability (Bottom-Left): A pie chart showing the health mix of your ecosystem (Optimized vs. Clogged vs. Turbulent).
   5. Strategic Summary (Bottom-Right): A concise text diagnostic that identifies the top bottleneck and recommends the next strategic move (Scale, Fix, or Re-design).

  1. Is this conversion drop an anomaly or "business as usual"?
   * Metric: Divergence (Consensus vs. Inertia).
   * The Answer: It distinguishes whether a drop-off is part of the funnel's inherent difficulty (Inertia) or if something broke recently (Negative Divergence). This
     allows you to identify bugs or broken UX in minutes rather than days.

  2. Is right now the perfect time to scale the budget?
   * Metric: Divergence > 0.1 + Consensus > 0.7.
   * The Answer: It identifies when a specific funnel stage is "heating up"—meaning current efficiency is outperforming historical norms. It tells you: "Scale now; the
     system is processing users more effectively than ever before."

  3. Is this a Product (UX) problem or a Marketing (Traffic) problem?
   * Metric: Difficulty vs. Consensus.
   * The Answer: If the difficulty is high across the board (Low Consensus), your UX/Product step is likely broken. If the difficulty is only high for specific groups, you
     are sending the wrong traffic to that stage of the funnel.

  4. How predictable is our ROI for the next month?
   * Metric: Status: STABLE.
   * The Answer: If the status is "Stable," divergence is minimal, meaning user behavior is consistent with history. You can trust your revenue projections. If it is
     "Turbulent," your ROI forecasts are high-risk and volatile.

  5. Which specific bottleneck deserves my attention today?
   * Metric: Action: RE-DESIGN.
   * The Answer: It automatically filters through all transition points to pinpoint the exact stage where resistance is highest. It prioritizes your Conversion Rate
